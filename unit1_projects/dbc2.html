The Footing and the Swing

For the most part you want to reach the ball in a very comfortable position. You'll want to be sideways so you can twist your torso while hitting the ball for an increased range of motion. For the swing, you want to make sure you are winding the racquet before the ball has arrived but not before your opponent has hit the ball.

After making contact with the ball you have to make sure the racquet follows it course. This is important for the trajectory of the ball as weird as it may sound.
